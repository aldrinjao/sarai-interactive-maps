SVTR Forecast based on April 2019 data

SVTR was developed by the DCAF Project to detect and assess local agricultural drought. This drought indicator follows the ratio of NDVI and LST which enables assessment of how the seasonal and interannual changes in the vegetation are related to changes in surface temperature.
