SVTR Map November 2018

The Standardized Vegetation-Temperature Ratio (SVTR), a novel drought index for the Philippines, was developed by Drought and Crop Assessment and Forecasting (DCAF) Project, to detect and assess local agricultural drought (Perez et al., 2016). This drought indicator follows the ratio of NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index), which represents the photosynthetic capacity of vegetation, and LST (Land Surface Temperature), which enables assessment of how the seasonal and interannual changes in the vegetation are related to changes in surface temperature.
